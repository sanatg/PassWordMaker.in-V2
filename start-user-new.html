Services and Features

Secure Data Encryption

We at PassWordMaker have implemented AES-256 bit encryption with PBKDF2 SHA-512 and salted hashes to ensure complete security in the cloud. You’ll create a password manager account with an email address and a strong master password (or social login though we recommend email and password) to locally-generate a unique encryption key

Unlimited Data Storage

Your data is encrypted and decrypted at the device level or client level. Data stored in your vault is kept secret, even from PassWordMaker. Your master password, and the keys used to encrypt and decrypt data, are never sent to PassWordMaker’ servers, and are never accessible by PassWordMaker.This means you are in good hands and all of your data too.

Always Free And Open

Get the highest security standards with a global community become a PassWordMaker user and improve the code repo.PasWordMaker also offers Secure and ability to share sensitive data within your PassWordMaker Vault from any browser, mobile or desktop,including windows,linux and mac os
